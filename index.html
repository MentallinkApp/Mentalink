Project Title: MentaLink - Mental Health Support via SMS and Voice

Project Description:

MentaLink is a mental health support platform designed to provide accessible, immediate support and resources through SMS and voice calls. This solution aims to bridge the gap in mental health services by offering discreet, easy-to-use communication channels for individuals seeking mental health support.

Key Features:

  • Anonymous Mental Health Chat via SMS: Users can initiate anonymous conversations with mental health professionals through SMS, ensuring privacy and confidentiality.
  • Mental Health Resources and Tips: Regularly scheduled SMS messages containing mental health tips, coping strategies, and information about local mental health services.
  • Voice-Based Crisis Hotline: Users can call a dedicated hotline for immediate support during a mental health crisis.
  • Mood Tracking and Journaling via SMS: Users can log their mood and significant daily events through SMS prompts, with periodic summaries and insights provided by the system.

Technology Stack:

  • Backend: Node.js/Express for API endpoints, MongoDB for data storage, Africa's Talking APIs for SMS and Voice services.
  • Frontend: Basic web interface for mental health professionals to manage conversations and view user data, admin dashboard for configuring SMS content and managing user data securely.

Development Plan:

  1. Introduction Phase: Team introductions, role assignments, setup GitHub repository and Docker environment.

    • Day 2: Develop voice-based crisis hotline and integrate with backend.
    • Day 3: Implement mood tracking and journaling feature. Conduct end-to-end testing.
    • Continuous Integration setup with Travis CI for automated testing and deployment.
  3. Demonstration and Wrap-up Phase: Prepare a demo highlighting key features, present the impact and potential of the project, collect feedback for further improvement.
